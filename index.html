Super Everestinho

Super Everestinho is a third person 3D platformer game where you are challenged to overcome linear obstacles in 5 minutes.
Developed in Unity, blending 2D and 3D elements and awarded as the most voted game at the PUC Game Show 2023/1.

Evoverse - Mine, Fight & Earn Crypto

I had the opportunity to work on the Evoverse blockchain game project as a member of the Firegecko development team in partnership with Animvs. My main responsibilities included creating utility screens such as team editing, inventory, options, and credits, as well as implementing specific behaviors for certain cards and creating UI/UX elements.

Official Website

Space Mavericks

Space Mavericks is a multiplayer game that combines elements of classic artillery games (such as Worms and Angry Birds). I was mainly responsible for applying Addressables to the project, setting up Google Cloud to load the ships from the internet, creating a system that generates palettes of three colors that match, and creating items in Playfab that represent the users' ships.

itch.io

Flip Square

Flip Square is an infinite running game built in Unity, where the player must avoid obstacles or rotate the square to fit through them. The game features 300 skins that can be unlocked through coins collected during runs or by completing achievements. As the sole creator, I designed every aspect of the game from concept to launch on the Play Store in 2021.

Global Game Jam page

Overcrooked

Multiplayer game developed in Unity during the Global Game Jam CWB 2020. Inspired by Overcooked, players compete to repair cars faster than their opponents. It was awarded the Best Unity Game at the event, and the achievement was featured on the Unity forum in Section "Brothers in Curitiba".
